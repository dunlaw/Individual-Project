* the complexity of the project  
The report presents a well-defined and nuanced discussion regarding AI-generated 
content from prompts. It offers a thoughtful analysis of the alignment between the 
creator’s intent and the extent to which AI-generated content can fulfill that intent. 
However, the report lacks clarity regarding the specific game being proposed. While the 
analysis is technically sound, the discussion remains largely theoretical and would 
benefit from being more closely grounded in the practical aspects of the proposed 
project. 
The topic is timely and interesting. The complexity of the project is hard to judge 
because large parts of the report are addressed too superficially. Already the 
Introduction should much more concretely state the problem and motivate the 
approach to address it. The report promises a lot but it is unclear to me to what extent 
the goals can be achieved.
